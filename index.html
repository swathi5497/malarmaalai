Our Story

Malar Flower has been crafting beautiful floral garlands for over 15 years. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in decorative garlands.

We believe that every celebration deserves the beauty of fresh flowers. Our team of skilled artisans works tirelessly to create stunning garlands that add elegance and fragrance to your special moments.

From traditional temple garlands to contemporary wedding decorations, we offer a wide range of options to suit every occasion and budget. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of thousands of happy customers.
